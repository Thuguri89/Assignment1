Service Delivery

Technology has made a great role in marketing by enhancing access to provision of services globally. This includes ordering a product, tracking orders, seeking information about a product, checking accounts records and prices of a product and reaching customers and company through websites. Technology has bridged the geographical distance between a consumer and a company globally thus enabling consumers to purchase and receive products and services across the world. Through self-services activities, technology has led to many companies sacking their unskilled employees and replacing them with automation. Technology thus has enabled companies to invest salary of the unskilled employee in investing financial firms thus reducing the cost. Consumers on the other hand acquire knowledge about the price of a product through technology, which enhance bargaining of a product in a marketing place leading to reduction of cost. To enhance accessibility of services, technology has played a great role in using websites and smart phone apps such as, Nywele nyumbani, Jumia and Kilimall to deliver more services and products to more consumers. Consumers acquires more information about a product through the apps, they also engage in interactions with consumers about a product before purchasing thus creating a relationship and trust leading to more increase to the consumers. Technology has played a major role in enhancing interactions amongst people from different places in the world. This occurs through Companies using secure, reliable video platforms such as zoom and Google meet where people are able to have one on one eye contact meeting and discuss their agendas smoothly. Also during the Corona, pandemic university students were able to continue with their learning through the zoom meeting. Technology has facilitated people to access banking services such as creating their transaction anywhere at their comfort zone such as at home through the mobile banking. Technology has also enabled Western Union Money Transfer whereby people in Diaspora, U.S.A can send money to their relatives in Kenya. Western Union Money Transfer also enhances purchase of products in the developed world as their mode of payment.
